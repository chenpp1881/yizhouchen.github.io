Education

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science, Peking University (since September 2023), specializing in Computer Software and Theory. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dan Hao, an IEEE Fellow and Cheung Kong Scholar. My doctoral research focuses on software engineering, blockchain security, artificial intelligence, and formal verification.

Prior to joining Peking University, I received my Master's degree in Electronic Information (Software Engineering) from the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University (2020–2022), where I was supervised by Prof. Jin Liu.
